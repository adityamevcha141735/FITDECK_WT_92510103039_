Welcome to Gym Admin Panel

This is a modern, futuristic admin panel designed specifically for gym management. Built with cutting-edge technology and a sleek interface, it provides comprehensive tools for managing your fitness facility.

Member Management

Comprehensive member database with advanced search, filtering, and management capabilities. Track memberships, attendance, and member activities.

  • Member registration & profiles
  • Membership tracking
  • Attendance monitoring
  • Payment management

Equipment Management

Monitor and manage all gym equipment with real-time status updates, maintenance scheduling, and usage analytics.

  • Equipment inventory
  • Maintenance scheduling
  • Usage analytics
  • Status monitoring

Analytics & Reports

Advanced analytics and reporting tools to help you understand your gym's performance and make data-driven decisions.

  • Revenue tracking
  • Member analytics
  • Equipment usage reports
  • Performance metrics

Staff Management

Manage your gym staff with scheduling, payroll, and performance tracking features.

  • Staff scheduling
  • Payroll management
  • Performance tracking
  • Training records
