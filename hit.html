Benefits of High Intensity Training

Maximize Calorie Burn

HIT workouts continue to burn calories for hours after your session ends through the afterburn effect (EPOC).

Time Efficient

Get maximum results in minimal time. Most HIT workouts are 30 minutes or less but deliver exceptional results.

Build Lean Muscle

HIT stimulates muscle growth while burning fat, helping you achieve a toned, athletic physique.

HIT Workouts

Circuit Training

Perform exercises back-to-back with minimal rest, targeting different muscle groups.

Sample Circuit:

  • Kettlebell Swings (45 sec)
  • Push-ups (45 sec)
  • Box Jumps (45 sec)
  • Rest (30 sec)

EMOM Workouts

Every Minute On the Minute - complete a set number of reps at the start of each minute.

Sample EMOM:

  • Min 1: 10 Thrusters
  • Min 2: 15 Calorie Row
  • Min 3: 20 Sit-ups
  • Repeat for 15-20 min

AMRAP Workouts

As Many Rounds As Possible - complete as many rounds of a circuit as you can in a set time.

Sample AMRAP (12 min):

  • 8 Pull-ups
  • 10 Push Presses
  • 12 Air Squats
